What I Did

  • Worked on 2D–3D grounding to connect image-based referent detection with object-linked XR overlays.
  • Implemented client-side XR overlay components that mapped detected referents to stable world-anchored visual targets.
  • Handled spatial alignment across image-space detection, camera projection, and world-space placement for object-grounded visual delivery.
  • Evaluated generated multimodal outputs for validity, contextual appropriateness, and alignment with intended response content.

Design Challenge

A key challenge was turning 2D referent detection from egocentric images into stable XR overlays that appeared on the intended real-world target. The visual cues were not generic screen-space annotations: they had to be grounded to physical referents, updated as the user moved, and remain consistent with model output timing.

This required bridging multiple coordinate spaces between image-based detection, camera projection, and world-space anchoring on the client side. I focused on the grounding and overlay side of that pipeline, especially where accurate object-linked placement was necessary for scene-aware visual delivery and evaluation.
